CONTENT ENTRY W/ ANALYST MINDSET - June 10, 2014

Application

What does it take to get to the next level, or in other words, the next phase of your life's satisfaction? What do you need to do?

Change, Adaptation & Opportunity

New experiences can be scary, challenging, difficult, but if you look at it from another perspective, say that of personal growth stemming from moving out of your hometown, meeting new people, or having motivation to do better on the daily basis, life can be very fulfilling. Some say winners grab opportunity, and even though the change and adaptation may not go the way you planned it, it's ok to learn, adjust, keep moving, and always keep going. That's life! New experiences and life stories are achievable when you take that first leap, or step into a direction you have yet to explore. So my question for you is what do you have to lose and what are you afraid of? Do you think you have the ability to change, adapt and scale all available opportunities to you?

Drive & Passion

What drives you? Are you satisfied with the life you currently have? If someone asked you what are you passionate about, how would you respond? What kind of hobbies are you interested in? What would be the first ideas, thoughts or words that come to your mind? Drive & Passion should consist of putting your strengths to work, preparing for the short term and long term.

Is That Your Final Decision?

Did you finish school? Did you apply for a job just to obtain some income? Is what you are doing enough to fulfill everything you want to do in this life? Everyone experiences life challenges differently. Many have taken the first necessary step to start the change and discovered just what they were capable of. Possibly you could experience the same personal growth and experience to get you where you want to go in life. If you never try, you will always fail.

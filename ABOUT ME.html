About Me

I grew up in a military family which led me to graduating overseas at an international school. I then pursued an entrepreneur management program at a university but after finishing one semester, I knew I was ready for bigger experiences.

I wanted to serve my country and also get ahead by enlisting in the US Air Force. I had the opportunity to learn the concepts of "Wingman. Leader. Warrior.", completed technical training school and earned a Certificate in Communications Applications Technology. I enlisted for 4 years where I became an Intelligence analyst. I created pre-mission briefings and presentation updates to also producing and distributing accurately collected data reports and technical writing summaries in a time sensitive environment. But the main mentality and lessons I've learned was mission comes first and service before self.

Back to the civilian side and now as a Military Entrepreneur, I'm always constantly trying to improve myself as often as I can and the ability to help others however way I can is something I take pride in. After my chapter in active duty I realized I still have the opportunity to do so much more and I truly believe that the skills I've obtained throughout my life before, during and after my military service will allow me to be able to adjust, adapt and do well towards what's in store in my future.

It's great to have a 5 year plan and 10 year plan, but it is important to live life to the fullest by always reaching towards your own goals and dreams. With Bootstrapping my own startup and pursuing other personal endeavors, I can't wait to see what else is in store in this little world of mine. Connect with me and let's share stories.
